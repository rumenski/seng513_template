FAQ

Do I need a driver's license?

No you don’t need a driver’s license! Any drivers under 18 must have the waiver signed by their guardian.

Is go-karting safe?

Our company focuses on everyone’s safety and all racers are required to watch a safety briefing before getting on the track. Anyone who breaks these rules will be out of the track.

Is there a minimum height, age, or weight limit?

Drivers must be a minimum of 4 ft. tall, 12 years of age, and under 250 lbs.

Do I need to book a reservation?

We offer drop-ins on a first come first serve basis but we highly recommend booking a reservation as our slots fill up fast.

How long is the race?

Approximately 8 minutes long. First racer to complete 15 laps wins the race.

Can you wear flip flops?

No open-toed shoes allowed! Dry closed toed shoes free of any mud, dirt are required.

Can we buy alcohol at Shifters Canada?

We have a strict no alcohol policy before the race. Any person who is intoxicated is not allowed on the track.

Can we buy food at Shifters Canada?

Our food stands have a variety of snacks and refreshments available!
